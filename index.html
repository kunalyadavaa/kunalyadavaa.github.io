Proxmox Virtual Environment

Proxmox VE is the conductor, virtualizing your TrueNAS (network attached storage) and other VMs. TrueNAS, a dedicated VM, acts as your data center, securely storing information. Portainer, a container management tool, can be installed within a separate VM on Proxmox. This setup lets you virtualize storage (TrueNAS), run applications (VMs), and manage containers (Portainer) for a flexible and centralized system.
